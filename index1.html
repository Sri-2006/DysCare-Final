The Struggle with Dyslexia and Dysgraphia

Dyslexia and Dysgraphia are learning differences that affect millions worldwide, making reading and writing incredibly challenging. These conditions impact academic performance, self-esteem, and overall well-being.

Empowering Literacy Through AI

DysCare is an app which helps you detect Dyslexia and Dysgraphia using AI features like eye tracking, handwriting detection, speech to text(for verbal communication challenges) and much more.

POSITIVE IMPACT

One in five individuals has dyslexia, and many struggle with decoding words, spelling, and handwriting. Early identification and intervention are crucial for unlocking the potential of individuals with dyslexia and dysgraphia, providing them with the tools and support they need to thrive.
